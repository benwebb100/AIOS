# Strategy — AutoOS

## North Star
**5 clients on retainer at $2,500/mo average = $12,500/mo recurring**, within 6 months of launch.

## Targets
- **First 3 clients within 60 days of launch** (founding tier: $1,500 setup + $1,000/mo)
- **Clients #4–5 at standard pricing** by month 4–6 ($3,000–5,000 setup + $2,000–3,000/mo)
- **$8–12K MRR** by end of month 3
- **$12.5K MRR** by end of month 6

## KPIs (from Master Playbook)
| KPI | Target |
|---|---|
| Away-from-desk autonomy (client can run their business from phone) | 90%+ within 60 days of setup |

## Acquisition Plan

> Diverges from Master Playbook intentionally: warm outreach first, then paid. The playbook's Meta-ads-first plan stays valid as the scaling motion — we're shifting *order*, not killing the channel.

### Phase 1 — Warm Outreach (current)
**Goal:** First 1–3 founding clients from Bailey's existing network. These are prototype clients — we're using them to stress-test the model, the delivery process, and the templates before spending on acquisition.

- Map Bailey's existing network: business owners, ex-colleagues, gym contacts, friends-of-friends running businesses
- Direct outreach: DMs, emails, in-person conversations — no funnel, no landing page required yet
- Pitch is conversational, not adversarial: "I'm building this thing, you'd be a perfect first client, founding pricing, want to chat?"
- **Founding-tier framing:** explicitly position as "case study + feedback partner" — they get the discount, we get the iterations
- Target: book 5–10 informal conversations → convert 1–3 to founding clients
- Use the prototype phase to: refine the discovery flow, build out the template library, iterate on the daily brief, capture testimonials

### Phase 2 — Meta Ads (after warm-outreach prototype clients)
Kicks in once the warm-outreach clients have validated the model and we have at least one strong case study to point to.
- Budget: **$30–50/day** to start
- Test 3–4 ad angles for 7 days each
- Kill anything > $50 CPL, scale anything < $20 CPL
- Geography: AU first, expand UK/US once a creative is winning

### Phase 3 — Content + Referrals
Runs alongside Phase 2 once we have live clients.
- LinkedIn posts: real client setups (anonymised if needed), before/after format
- Screen recordings of AIOS features in action
- Client testimonials (video > text)
- Referral incentive: **$500 credit** for each referred client who signs

### Phase 4 — Partnerships (month 3+)
- Business coaches / consultants (offer AIOS to their clients)
- Accountants / bookkeepers (their clients = our ICP)
- Marketing agencies (white-label or referral)

## Ad Angles to Test (Phase 2)
1. **Overwhelm** — "AI is everywhere and you're still copy-pasting into ChatGPT. There's a better way."
2. **Time** — "What if AI actually knew your business and could work on it without you explaining everything?"
3. **ROI** — "Business owners using an AI operating system save 15–20 hours a week. Here's what that looks like."
4. **FOMO** — "Your competitors are setting up AI systems that run their businesses. You're still using ChatGPT for email drafts."
5. **Demo** — Screen recording of a real AIOS in action.

## Sales Process (high-level)
1. **Phase 1:** Warm outreach → conversation → discovery call. **Phase 2+:** Ad → Landing Page → Calendly booking.
2. Discovery call (30 min) — see `SOPs/Discovery-Call.md`
3. Same-day proposal — see `SOPs/Sales-Process.md`
4. Setup session (2–4 hrs) — Layers 0–2 installed
5. Week 1 daily check-ins
6. Monthly retainer kicks in — 1–2 automations / month + ongoing

## Vertical Strategy
**Don't pick a niche yet.** Take the first 5 clients from wherever they come. The niche will reveal itself based on which clients get the most value and which ones Bailey enjoys working with. Master Playbook Part 7 has the tier ranking.

## Tracked Metrics (track weekly)
- **Phase 1:** Warm conversations had → discovery calls booked → founding clients closed
- **Phase 2+:** Cost per lead (Meta Ads), discovery call booking rate
- Close rate (conversation/call → paying client)
- Client retention / monthly churn
- Revenue per client (setup + retainer + upsells)
- Time per client per month (Bailey's capacity)
