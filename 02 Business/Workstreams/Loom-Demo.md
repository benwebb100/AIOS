## Two versions

### Version A — Dogfooded (can film immediately)
Bailey's own AutoOS workspace (this very vault) running:

**Risk:** Bailey's own AIOS may not feel "client-shaped" — it's the agency, not a generic business. Could read as "of course this works for AI consultants."

**Mitigation:** Frame the workflow as universal (ingesting a meeting, updating client records, drafting follow-ups). Bailey the operator > Bailey the AI consultant in how it's narrated.

### Version B — Client result (waits for Lou or Jason)
Same recording style, but on a real client workspace. Stronger proof, harder to film (privacy, timing, polish, anonymisation).
