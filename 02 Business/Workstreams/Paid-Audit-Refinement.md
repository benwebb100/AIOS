## Brainstorm Topics (between Bailey and Ben/Claude)
- **Pricing band.** $250–500 founding tier. Should it be flat $500, or $250 anchor → $500 standard? Trigger condition for the move to $1K+?
- **Cookbook depth.** Current template has Executive Summary + Technical Detail. Right split, or does it need a third tier (one-pager for the owner, deck for the team)?
- **Interview structure.** 1 owner + 1–2 stakeholders. Sufficient for a $200K-revenue solopreneur? Sufficient for a $5M-revenue 20-person team? Should the band be variable?
- **Demo build time.** SOP estimates 3–4 hrs to build the demo AIOS for the presentation. With a template AIOS workspace, can this drop to 1 hr?
- **Drop-off filter.** Currently passive ("if they ghost during scheduling, they were never going to close"). Active 7-day "audit slot expires" frame instead?
- **Scope-creep defence.** Standard language for when the prospect tries to extract free advice during the audit interviews.
- **Audit → Architecture handoff.** Clean transition or a deliberate pause/decision moment? Frame as "you can stop here and the cookbook is yours"?
