# AIOS Agency — Shared Context

## What This Repo Is
Shared knowledge base for the AIOS Agency business. Two people work from this:
- **Ben** — Strategic advisor. Not hands-on delivery.
- **Bailey** — Operator. Runs sales, delivery, and client work.

## Key Files
- `context/About.md` — Business model, pricing, tech stack, roles
- `context/GTM.md` — Go-to-market, targeting, sales process, pricing tiers
- `reference/Master-Playbook.md` — Full playbook/constitution for the business
- `reference/Original-Brainstorm.md` — Original brainstorm (historical context)

## Rules
- When working on AIOS strategy or content, load `context/About.md` and `context/GTM.md` first
- The Master Playbook is the constitution — it has the full framework, delivery process, and templates
- Update `context/` docs when business decisions change (pricing, positioning, new verticals, etc.)
- Keep docs concise and current — update in place, don't append chronologically
