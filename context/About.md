# AIOS Agency — Project Context

## What This Is
An AI Operating System (AIOS) setup and management agency run by Bailey, with Ben advising on strategy. The business installs AI operating systems for business owners — contextualising Claude Code with their entire business, connecting their tools, and building automations that free up their bandwidth.

## Business Model

### Pricing (Under Review — See Webinar Intel)
Current playbook pricing is likely too low based on market intel (Eric Bledsoe doing $200K+/mo at $10K first seat). Pricing needs recalibrating before launch.

- **Paid Audit (NEW — replaces free discovery call):** $500–1,500 for initial business audit
  - Interview stakeholders, document workflows, map current → future state
  - Delivers plain-English "cookbook" + live demo presentation
  - The audit IS the close — "puppy dog close" (let them hold it)
- **Architecture (AIOS Foundation):** TBD — playbook said $1,500–5,000, market says $5,000–10,000+ (Eric is US with 25yrs credibility — our AU founding pricing will be lower)
- **Per-seat pricing for teams:** ~$2,500 per additional employee
- **Modular add-ons:** individual automations/solutions sold as toggle-on/off extras
- **Retainer vs Cash Upfront:** market leans toward high-ticket upfront; retainer is optional for ongoing ops

### Revenue Model Options (Decide Before Launch)
1. **Retainer model (original playbook):** lower setup + monthly recurring
2. **High-ticket upfront (Eric's model):** large cash infusion, modular add-ons over time
3. **Hybrid:** paid audit → architecture setup → optional retainer for ongoing builds

### Roles
- **Bailey:** All sales, discovery, setup, delivery, and ongoing builds
- **Ben:** Strategic advisor, playbook designer, async coaching (not hands-on delivery)

## Our Position (Context for Strategy Decisions)
- Ben (21) and Bailey (20) — young, no deep business networks, no decades of corporate relationships
- Based in Australia — market is slower to adopt than US, but less competition
- Bailey has real technical skills (Claude Code, Supabase, Render, Git) — but these are insurance, NOT the product
- Eric Bledsoe's model works because of 25 years of business credibility and in-person trust. We adapt the *principles* (trust-first, paid discovery, sell outputs) to channels that work for us (LinkedIn content, coworking spaces, startup meetups, online communities, warm outreach)
- **Key risk:** Bailey's builder instinct. Technical skills make it tempting to build custom tools instead of focusing on context engineering and outputs. Every hour coding a custom app is an hour not spent on the thing that actually creates client value.

## Origin
Bailey was originally building a dental Voice AI agency (forked from SalesPro AI). Pivoted to AIOS because:
1. No distribution partner for Voice AI (Ben has Steve/ScanWise for gyms — Bailey had no equivalent)
2. AIOS has more upside potential and forward trajectory
3. Bailey's existing tech skills (Claude Code, Supabase, Render, Git) transfer directly
4. Market timing is ideal — business owners are AI-curious but overwhelmed

## Delivery Stack
- **Claude Code** — Bailey's build tool for context engineering and automation development
- **Claude Desktop App (Cowork)** — the client-facing interface (NOT a custom dashboard)
- **Obsidian** — persistent knowledge base for each client's business context (back-of-house)
- **MCP Servers** — connect Claude to client tools (CRM, email, calendar, analytics, etc.)
- **GitHub** — version control for any code/config
- **Supabase / Render** — only if client needs hosted services or data layers

### Client Interface Decision (Resolved)
**No custom dashboard.** Clients interact via Claude's native desktop app. Market proof: Eric Bledsoe does $200K+/mo delivering via Claude Cowork with zero custom UI. The value is in context engineering and outputs, not a dashboard. Dashboard deferred indefinitely — revisit only if multiple clients independently request visibility features. See `reference/Council-Verdict-Dashboard-Timing.md`.
