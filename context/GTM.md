# AIOS Agency — Go-to-Market

## Target Market
Business owners and operators who:
- Already use ChatGPT/Claude (AI-curious, early adopter mindset)
- Run knowledge-heavy or service-based businesses
- Are the bottleneck in their own business (their time/knowledge is the constraint)
- Revenue $200K–$5M (big enough to justify the spend, small enough that they're hands-on)

## Positioning
NOT "AI automation agency" (commoditised, vague, associated with zapier/make.com point solutions).

Instead: "We set up an AI operating system that actually knows your business — your team, your clients, your numbers, your processes — and runs it with you."

The differentiator: this isn't another chatbot or Zapier workflow. It's a persistent AI brain for the business that compounds over time.

## Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: Meta Ads (Primary Channel)
- Target: business owners, founders, operators, consultants, agency owners
- Interest targeting: entrepreneurship, business coaching, productivity, AI tools
- Age: 28–55
- Geography: AU initially (Bailey's network), expand to UK/US

#### Ad Angles to Test
1. **The overwhelm angle:** "AI is everywhere and you're still copy-pasting into ChatGPT. There's a better way."
2. **The time angle:** "What if AI actually knew your business — your clients, your numbers, your processes — and could work on it without you explaining everything?"
3. **The ROI angle:** "Business owners using an AI operating system are saving 15–20 hours a week. Here's what that looks like."
4. **The FOMO angle:** "Your competitors are setting up AI systems that run their businesses. You're still using ChatGPT for email drafts."
5. **The demo angle:** Video of a real AIOS in action — "This is what an AI operating system looks like for a [type] business."

#### Ad Format
- Short-form video (30–60 sec) — Bailey talking to camera or screen recording of AIOS in action
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- CTA: "Book a free strategy call" → Calendly link
- Landing page: simple, one-page, above-fold CTA

### Phase 2: Content + Referrals (After First 3 Clients)
- LinkedIn posts documenting real client setups (before/after)
- Screen recordings of AIOS features (daily brief, automations, data pulls)
- Client testimonials
- Referral incentive: $500 credit for each referred client who signs up

### Phase 3: Partnerships (Month 3+)
- Business coaches / consultants who want to offer this to their clients
- Accountants / bookkeepers who serve small business owners
- Marketing agencies who want to add AI services without building capability

## Sales Process
1. **Ad → Landing Page → Book Call** (automated)
2. **Discovery Call (30 min)** — understand their business, tools, pain points (follow constitution)
3. **Proposal** — tailored scope based on discovery, sent same day
4. **Setup Session (2–4 hours)** — remote or in-person, Layers 0–2 installed
5. **Week 1 check-in** — daily brief live, first automation running
6. **Monthly retainer begins** — 1–2 automations per month + ongoing optimisation

## Pricing Strategy
- **Founding clients (first 3):** $1,500 setup + $1,000/mo — explicitly framed as founding pricing
- **Standard pricing:** $3,000–5,000 setup + $2,000–3,000/mo
- **Premium (in-person setup):** Add $1,000–2,000 to setup for travel + in-person delivery
- Annual commitment discount: 2 months free on 12-month retainer

## Key Metrics to Track
- Cost per lead (Meta Ads)
- Discovery call booking rate
- Close rate (call → paying client)
- Client retention (monthly churn)
- Revenue per client (setup + retainer + upsells)
- Time per client per month (Bailey's capacity constraint)
