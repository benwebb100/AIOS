Is this just ChatGPT?

No. ChatGPT is the model. The AIOS is the system around it: your business as context, your tools connected, your processes running automatically. The model is one component of many.

What does the audit actually produce?

A "cookbook" — a document mapping your current state, your future state, and a prioritised list of automations. Plus a working AIOS prototype loaded with your context that you can interact with live. Yours to keep regardless of what we do next.

What about my data?

Your knowledge base lives in your accounts — Google Drive, Notion, Obsidian, your choice. We connect Claude to it via official integrations. Nothing gets uploaded to third-party black boxes.

What happens if I want to stop?

You keep everything. The vault, the integrations, the workflows. The system was always yours — we just installed it.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see 60–70% of recurring tasks automated within 30–60 days of the setup session. The longer you use it, the more it compounds.

Who's a fit?
